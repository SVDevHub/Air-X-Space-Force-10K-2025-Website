Air X Space Force 10K 2025

Running Below. Reaching Beyond

Date & Time: Saturday January 6, 2026 at 8am.

Location: 2354 Fairchild Dr Suite 3A15, Air Force Academy, Colorado 80840.

About the Event

The Air X Space Force 10K 2025 is a collaborative running event that celebrates unity, strength, and a shared mission between two cutting edge branches of the U.S. military. Designed to showcase mutual support for their respective causes, this event brings together service members, veterans, and civilians in a high energy 10K open to the public. Whether you're running in uniform or alongside military supporters, participants will honor the values of excellence, resilience, and innovation. This event is more than a race; it is a symbol of air and space power moving forward together.

What to Expect

A great time for everyone

  • Breathtaking Course: Run a challenging yet inspiring 10K through the prestigious U.S. Air Force Academy, featuring mountain views, crisp air, and historic landmarks
  • Post-Race Party: Celebrate your accomplishment with fellow runners at a high-energy gathering on base, complete with music, games, and patriotic spirit
  • Free Food: Enjoy complimentary snacks and refreshments at the finish line, including protein-packed options to help you recharge and recover.
